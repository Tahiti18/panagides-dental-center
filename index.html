Our Dentists

Guided by specialized clinical credentials and continuous education.

DDS - Dental Surgeon

Dr. Adonis Panagidis

Dr. Panagidis was born in Larnaca, Cyprus, in 1982. In 2002, he entered the Dental School of the National and Kapodistrian University of Athens, Greece, graduating in 2008 as a Dental Surgeon.

After practicing general dentistry in Nicosia for 12 years, in March 2020 he founded his own state-of-the-art Dental Center to provide top-tier, technology-driven oral healthcare.

POSTGRADUATE CREDENTIALS

Curriculum Implantology (2010): Completed intensive training in Berlin and Athens, receiving the Certificate Curriculum Implantology from the University of Cologne in Germany.

Aesthetic Dentistry (2014): Completed an intensive Clinical Program in Aesthetic Dentistry in Athens, Greece.

Soft Tissue Management (2016): Participated in specialized hands-on clinics on tissue health around implants and natural teeth.

ITI Member: Active member of the International Team for Implantology, studying global standards in implant design.
